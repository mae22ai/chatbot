endings of Korean and to evaluate their meaning to the status
of ending. To accomplish this purpose, I investigate the
distribution of ending on the base of corpus linguistics, so I
delimited the endings to ‘-go, -eo/eoseo, -go/goseo, -(eu)myeon,

“conjunctive” circumstance and “terminative” circumstance. And

e) “#ha-”
